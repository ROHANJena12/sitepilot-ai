# Feature: Business Impact

## Responsibility

Owns framing of audit findings in business terms — estimated impact, risk/priority language, and stakeholder-oriented summaries derived from technical signals.

## Boundaries

- **In scope:** Impact summaries, priority callouts, and business-oriented copy modules.
- **Out of scope:** Technical recommendation lists (`features/recommendations`), health score math presentation (`features/health-score`), and shared formatters (`shared/utils`).

## Composition

Typically composed inside report views and executive summary widgets.

## Status

Scaffold only. Implementation pending.
