# Feature: Health Score

## Responsibility

Owns presentation and interaction around a website’s overall health score — score breakdown, trend cues, and drill-downs into contributing signals.

## Boundaries

- **In scope:** Health score display modules and score-section interactions.
- **Out of scope:** Raw audit entity data (`entities/audit`), business impact framing (`features/business-impact`), and chart widgets (`widgets/charts`).

## Composition

Typically composed inside report and dashboard surfaces.

## Status
